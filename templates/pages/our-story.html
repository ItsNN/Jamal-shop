Our Story

From the heart of our vast desert and within the depths of our rich Saudi heritage, camels have always symbolized pride and livelihood. They are more than just a mode of transportation, serving as a token of hospitality, a source of health benefits, and a boost for immunity against illnesses. It is only natural, therefore, to return to our roots and embrace camel milk once more, a staple from our ancestors' times, noted for its plethora of health benefits and myriad nutrients. This prompted us to question: is camel milk merely an aspect of our cultural heritage?

Could it possibly be a significant dietary staple on Saudi tables today? From this point, we drew inspiration from the "Jamal" concept to disseminate the culture of camel milk and market its varied high-quality products. Just as our forebears utilized "Jamal" for water, today we employ it to provide fresh, natural camel milk products, free from added phosphates or cane sugar, within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and across global markets.

Our Mission

Offer “Saudi-made” innovative camel dairy products coupled with an outstanding consumer experience and enabled by state-of-the-art operations and facilities.

Environmental responsibility

Jamal is committed to elevating the environmental, social, and governance (ESG) standards in the Kingdom, aligning with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals in every facet of its business operations. Jamal strives to elevate the camel milk industry within the Kingdom by adopting the principles of quality and sustainability while fostering innovation to produce products that provide value to consumers. Moreover, it reinforces the Kingdom's endeavor to diversify income sources, adhering to Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 objectives and its ambition for a diversified and sustainable national economy.

Our Vision

To be the world’s leading camel dairy brand known for its unwavering commitment to promote Saudi heritage and sustainability across the value chain.
